About

Welcome to my website! I am an applied microeconomist and an Assistant Professor at the University of Victoria in British Columbia, Canada. I graduated from the University of Minnesota in 2022 with a hD in Public Affairs. My fields are: labor economics, public economics, and economic history, with a focus on North America. Before discovering economics and public policy, I studied and worked in wildlife genetics.
