About

I am an applied microeconomist and an Assistant Professor at the University of Victoria in British Columbia, Canada. I received my PhD in Public Affairs from the University of Minnesota in 2022. My research fields include labor economics, public economics, and economic history, with a particular focus on North America. Before turning to economics and public policy, I studied and worked in wildlife genetics.

Research Interests

  • Economics and political economy of occupational regulation
  • Economics of wildlife management
